Technology is my passion,
and I have spent my career exploring this field as a student and a teacher.

Software development has given me a unique opportunity to work on applications that improve lives, inspire creativity, and connect the world together. Who can ask for more?

What makes it so appealing to those of us fortunate enough to have technology careers? It challenges even the best, frustrates all of us, and leaves us with immense satisfcation when that one problem you have been hunting for hours, or days, finally reveals a solution.

DRIVEN

Unlike many developers, I spent the first part of my technology career in IT operations. I have personally configured, tested, deployed, and managed software platforms for large enterprises down to small businesses.

My career has spanned multiple industries including healthcare, manufacturing, finance, technology, and education.

FOCUSED

Drive requires focus. I engineer software that is intuitive to users, simple in design, and effective in use. Code should be clean and efficient. I will settle for nothing less.

I have experienced firsthand how well-developed software empowers users, enhances business processes, and eases the lives of those tasked to deploy it. I also know how poorly-developed software can impede a business and its users from reaching their full potential.

UNIQUE

Learning is my fuel. Developers must be simultaneously teacher and student, or we risk falling behind. If I am not working on my latest project, you may find me teaching a class, or simply diving into something new.

Because of my well-rounded career, I can easily relate to end-users, IT professionals, and business leaders on all levels. The software I develop are simply 'tools for the people,' and we need to know our audience.

"We keep moving forward, opening new doors, and doing new things, because we're curious and curiosity keeps leading us down new paths."

~ Walt Disney ~
